Leadership Profile

Led by Hareesh Sir — B.Tech, B.Sc., M.Sc. (Physics)

Abhay Coaching Centre is maintained and managed under the strict values of the Abhay Educational Trust (AP Government Regd No. 113/2021). The centre's foundational rule, conceptualized by director Hareesh Sir, remains disciplinary education, result orientation.

We avoid traditional memory drills, focusing instead on deep mathematical proofs, physical experiments, and biological structure drawings to ensure conceptual clarity.

Our Mission

To ensure student growth from primary schooling to pre-university levels through individual classroom attention and conceptual instruction methods.

Our Vision

To act as Santhapet's most trusted coaching center for high board grades and competitive pre-foundations (JEE / NEET / EAPCET).

Our Values

Ensuring complete code of conduct and discipline in study batches, conceptual instruction clarity, and maintaining separate classrooms for girls.
